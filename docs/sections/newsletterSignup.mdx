# Introduction on Newsletter Signup

The `Newsletter Signup` section allows visitors to subscribe to WWK newsletter by entering their email.

It display a simple from with a title,placeholder text input,successfully message.



## Basic use
To use the `Newsletter Signup` section:

    - Click on **Add Section** in the CMS.
    - Choose **Newsletter Signup** from the section list.
    - Fill in the required fields:
        - **Title**: The title of the form.
        - **Placeholder**: The placeholder text for the email input field.
        - **Success Message**: The message displayed when the form is successfully submitted.


![add section](https://wvn.hcm.ss.bfcplatform.vn/uploads/add-newletter.png)
